The Hu-Kno-Mian Oath

I take this oath, freely and sincerely

I will keep learning — and know better, so I may live better.

I will earn my living cleanly — good money, never at another's cost.

I will not hoard the light I am given — I will pass it on.

And when I have risen, even a little, I will reach back a hand — so no one beside me walks the road alone.
